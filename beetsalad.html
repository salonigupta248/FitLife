Beet Power Mason Jar Salad

Ingredients

1 cup shredded carrots
8 cooked beets, chopped or shredded
2 cups cooked quinoa
1 cup shredded brussels sprouts
½ cup golden raisins
½ cup crumbled goat cheese
¼ cup roasted salted cashews
8 cups baby spinach
Garlic Tahini Dressing


Calories: 379kcal Carbohydrates: 49g Protein: 19g
Fat: 14g Fiber: 12g Sugar: 14g

Instructions



  1. 1. Cook your quinoa according to the package instructions if it's not already cooked.
  2. 2. If your vegetables aren’t chopped or shredded already, prep them!
  3. 3. Make the dressing by blending together all the ingredients.
  4. 4. For the mason jars: Line up 4 32 ounce wide-mouth mason jars. Add 3 Tablespoons of dressing into each jar then start layering ingredients in this order: carrots, beets, quinoa, raisins, goat cheese, cashews, brussels sprouts and baby spinach.
  5. 5. Place lid on jars and keep in fridge until ready to serve.
  6. 6. When ready to enjoy, shake jar and pour into a serving bowl.
