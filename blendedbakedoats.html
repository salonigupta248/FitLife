Blended Baked Oats

Ingredients

½ cup rolled or quick oats
¼ cup unsweetened almond milk
½ ripe banana
1 egg*
1 Tablespoon maple syrup, I used monkfruit maple syrup*
1 Tablespoon peanut butter*
¼ teaspoon baking powder
Pinch of cinnamon
Pinch of sea salt
2 Tablespoons chocolate chips,


Calories: 503kcal Carbohydrates: 71g Protein: 18g
Fat: 25g Saturated Fat: 8g Polyunsaturated Fat: 10g Cholesterol:186mg Sodium: 419mg Fiber: 17g Sugar: 11g

Instructions



  1. 1. Preheat Oven: Preheat oven to 350°F.
  2. 2. Blend: Add all ingredients except for the chocolate chips into a blender and blend until fully combined. Stir in 1-2 Tablespoons of chocolate chips.
  3. 3. Bake: Pour batter into greased baking dish or ramekin. I used an 8 oz mini Le Creuset, but any 8 oz oven-safe dish will work. Top with extra chocolate chips and bake at 350°F for 25 minutes or until an inserted toothpick comes out clean.
  4. 4. Serve: Let cool, top with an extra drizzle of peanut butter, maple syrup and a splash of milk and dive in.
